## The Orchestrator
The `project-orchestrator` plugin orchestrated the development of this codebase across multiple phases.

## Subagents & Roles
- **Lead Dev**: Responsible for TDD architecture, failing specs, and interface definitions.
- **Junior Dev**: Responsible for the implementation of the specifications.
- **QA Engineer**: Responsible for adversarial testing, structural integrity, and integration tests ensuring >80% coverage.
- **Documentator**: Responsible for internal architectural logs and generating the `docs/whitepaper.rst`.
